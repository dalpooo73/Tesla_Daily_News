Who is Tesla?

Tesla is a transportation and energy company. It sells vehicles under its ‘Tesla Motors’ division and stationary battery packs for home, commercial and utility-scale projects under its ‘Tesla Energy’ division. The firm acquired its sister-company, solar installer SolarCity, in 2016. It is now part of its energy division. The company is led by Elon Musk, who currently hold the roles of CEO, Co-founder, Chairman, and Product Architect.
